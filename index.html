About Us

Mission:Impizzable is a personal business founded by our owner, Tom Luther, who after coming from Italy, decided to start this business as an April Fool's Joke, against the wishes of several Italians. Due to this, the kitchen staff are referred to as 'Impizzanators'. The pizzas offered are crafted to the customer's liking, since the menu only serves as a selection of presets. For regular customers, their custom-made pizzas are saved as 'Impizzanations', which can only be ordered by said regular customer. Welcome to try our special, the Mona Pizza, said to be first made by Da Vinci himself.

Mission:Impizzable HQ is in Buruburu, 500 Metres east of Tuskys Eastlands, opposite Just Meno. Delivery services are available, and if the pizza arrives late, we offer in on the house as an apology.
